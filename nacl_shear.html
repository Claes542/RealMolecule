NaCl shear modulus — strain-controlled

Small NaCl crystallite (3×3×3 ions). Top y-layer is frozen and displaced in x by ε·Ly; bottom y-layer is frozen at original positions. Middle y-layer is free. After RealQM convergence, the sum of x-forces on the displaced top atoms gives the shear stress σxy = ΣFx / (Lx Lz). Repeat for several ε; G = slope of σxy vs ε.

Reference: NaCl C44 ≈ 12.6 GPa (single-crystal, 300 K). Polycrystalline G ≈ 14.7 GPa.
