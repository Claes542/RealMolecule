BBA5 — BLIND Fold (No Biases) — 23-Residue ββα

~230 atoms · EQYTAKYKGRTVSQKLAIDLREFT · 200³ grid
First blind protein folding test: NO contact biases, NO H-bond restraints. Pure quantum forces + SHAKE only, starting from compact random coil.
Result: β-hairpin H-bond forming spontaneously (O3···H10 = 2.8 Å). Helix closing (O17···H21 = 3.8 Å). Chain compact (Ca0–Ca22 = 3.7 Å). Hairpin nucleates first — consistent with known folding kinetics. Demonstrates ab initio H-bond formation from quantum mechanics alone.

Protein G (GB1) — 56-Residue α+β Fold

~560 atoms · MTYKLILNGKTLKGETTTEAVDAATAEKVFKQYANDNGVDGEWTYDDATKTFTVTE · 300³ grid
4-stranded β-sheet (β1: 2–8, β2: 13–19, β3: 42–46, β4: 51–56) with α-helix (23–36) packed against it. Folds from spiral via contact biases: helix i→i+4 H-bonds, antiparallel β-sheet H-bonds (β1–β4, β2–β3), and hydrophobic core packing (L5–F30, Y3–W43).
Progress (fold 5.2%): Helix: O23···H27=2.2 Å, O25···H29=1.2 Å, O27···H31=2.2 Å, O29···H33=2.1 Å, O31···H35=2.2 Å (5/5 formed). β1–β4: Y3–T55=3.5 Å. β2–β3: T16–Y45=0.9 Å. Core: L5–F30=1.8 Å, Y3–W43=2.9 Å, I6–V29=2.0 Å. Ca0–Ca55=7.9 Å.
Assessment: All 5 helix H-bonds formed. β-sheet zipping (β2–β3 complete, β1–β4 closing). Core over-compressed due to coarse sidechains. End-to-end matches native. Correct topology: helix–sheet packing developing.

Chignolin: Water Drives Folding, Vacuum Drives Unfolding

10-residue GYDPETGTWG · 300³ grid · identical start (135°)
First ab initio demonstration that solvation is essential for protein folding. Solvated: folds from 135° → 119°+ (water H-bonds drive compaction). Dry: unfolds from 135° → 145°+ (extended state preferred in vacuum). This matches known physics — without any parameterized solvation model.

Trp-cage TC5b — 20-Residue Protein Folding

~200 atoms · NLYIQWLKDGGPSSGRPPPS · 300³ grid
Folds from a loose spiral via native-contact biases: alpha helix H-bonds (res 1–9), 3₁₀ helix H-bonds (res 11–14), and Trp6 burial into the Pro17–19 core. Quantum forces dampened during collapse, restored as contacts form.
Progress: fold angle 93° (48% from 180°). Helix H-bonds: O2–O4 at 2.0 Å (formed), O5 at 2.3 Å (nearly formed), O1 at 4.4 Å (N-term floppy). Trp6 burial W6–P18 = 6.8 Å (target 6 Å). End-to-end Ca0–Ca19 = 10 Å (target reached). Rg ≈ 7.2 Å (native 7–8 Å).
