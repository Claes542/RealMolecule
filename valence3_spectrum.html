Group-13 spectrum — 3-valence reduced kernel (closed shell + s² absorbed)

For B, Al, Ga, In, Tl: shell occupation [closed]·s²·p¹. The closed inner shell plus the inner s² pair are absorbed into a reduced kernel of charge +1, leaving only the outer p electron explicit. Same empty-core + Neumann model as the alkalis, applied to the ground p and excited s/p/d levels. Solve −½u″ + [ℓ(ℓ+1)/(2r²) − Z/r]u = Eu on r ∈ [r_c, R_max] and fit (Zkernel, r_c) to NIST.
