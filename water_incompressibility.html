Water incompressibility — bulk modulus K
P(ρ) from the virial → K = ρ dP/dρ, in REAL GPa
P (blue) vs density ρ. The slope gives the bulk modulus K = ρ dP/dρ, converted to GPa (1 Ha/au³ = 29422 GPa). Water K ≈ 2.2 GPa (nearly incompressible). Steep P(ρ) = stiff.
Caveats: confined droplet (some surface effect); reduced/under-bonded water; in RealQM much of the stiffness is electron exclusion (excluded volume), not H-bonding — an interesting distinction.
